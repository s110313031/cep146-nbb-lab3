# Tomato Basil Bruschetta

Yield: 6–8 servings\
Prep time: 15 minutes

![Tomato Basil Bruschetta](https://cookieandkate.com/images/2019/08/tomato-bruschetta-with-balsamic-drizzle.jpg)

## Ingredients

* 1 baguette, sliced into 1/2-inch pieces

* 2 cups cherry tomatoes, finely chopped

* 2 Tbsp olive oil (plus more for brushing bread)

* 1 clove garlic, minced

* 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped

* Salt and black pepper, to taste

* Optional: balsamic glaze or grated Parmesan

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).

1. Arrange baguette slices on a baking sheet and lightly brush with olive oil.

1. Bake 8–10 minutes until lightly golden and crisp.

1. In a bowl, combine tomatoes, olive oil, garlic, basil, salt, and pepper.

1. Spoon tomato mixture onto toasted bread slices.

1. Finish with balsamic glaze or Parmesan if desired. Serve immediately.

## Notes

* For extra flavor, rub the warm toast with a cut clove of garlic before topping.

* Can be prepared up to 1 hour ahead (store topping separately).
